Thamani

Transaction fee tracker for mobile money.

Every time you send money, pay a bill, or withdraw cash on M-Pesa, Airtel Money, or your bank, you pay a fee. Most people never add it up. Thamani reads your financial SMS messages and breaks down exactly what each transaction cost you: the fee, the percentage, and which provider is charging you the most. 100% on-device, zero cloud sync.

Thamani

The Problem

Mobile money is the lifeline of East Africa. Millions of people use M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and bank USSD services daily, but few realize how much they're spending on transaction fees alone. These fees are buried in SMS confirmations, and over a month they add up to a significant amount. There's no easy way to see the big picture of what you're actually paying to move your own money around.

How It Works

01

SMS Parsing

Thamani reads your financial SMS messages (M-Pesa, Airtel Money, bank notifications) using on-device SMS permissions. It extracts transaction amounts, fees, and provider details using pattern matching, no data ever leaves your phone.

02

Fee Breakdown

Each transaction is broken down into the actual amount sent/received and the fee charged. You see the exact percentage each provider takes, making it easy to compare costs across M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and bank transfers.

03

Analytics Dashboard

Monthly and weekly summaries show your total fees, average fee percentage, and which provider costs you the most. Visualizations help you spot trends and make smarter decisions about which service to use for different transaction types.

04

Privacy First

Zero cloud sync, zero tracking, zero third-party services. Everything is stored locally on your device using Room database. Your financial data stays yours, always.
